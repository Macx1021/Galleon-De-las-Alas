About Us

We are happy to serve you since day 1.

Chickanoks was built since 1998 in Puerto Princesa Palawan. It serves different chicken dishes. The restaurant was owned by Mark Rodney Galleon and Dinoh De las Alas. They decided to make this business bacause they are both chicken lovers and they want every kind of chicken dishes. Now there are 24 branches of Chickanoks all over the Philippines and it is continously growing.
